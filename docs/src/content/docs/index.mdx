## How it Works

<Steps>

1. **You ask a question:** The AI receives your complex architectural request.
2. **Cortex Vectors it:** The AI searches your local markdown files instantly using `sqlite-vec`.
3. **Cortex Graphs it:** The AI cross-references the result against your Domain-Driven topological graph to find dependencies.

</Steps>

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="Enforce Boundaries" icon="setting">
